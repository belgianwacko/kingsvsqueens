👑 How to Play

Fill an 8×8 board by placing 8 Queens and 2 Kings. Each puzzle has exactly one solution.

♛ Queens

  • Place one queen in each colored region (estate).
  • No two queens may share the same row or column.
  • Queens cannot be adjacent to each other (including diagonals) — but they can share a long diagonal.

♚ Kings

  • Both kings go on gray cells only.
  • Kings cannot be adjacent to or on the same diagonal as any queen (at any distance).
  • Kings cannot share a row, column, or diagonal with each other.

💡 Hints

Stuck? Tap the Hint button — it won't just tell you the answer, it'll explain why.

  • Each tap reveals a little more: first a nudge, then the reasoning, then the answer.
  • Hints walk you through the exact logic — which cells conflict, which estates are confined, what chain of deductions leads to the elimination.
  • For longer chains, steps are revealed one at a time so you can follow along.
💡 Don't be afraid to use hints! They act as an in-game tutorial — each one teaches you a solving technique you can apply on your own next time.

📊 Difficulty

25 handcrafted puzzles across five tiers — from gentle introductions to fiendish chain-reasoning challenges.

  • Beginner — queens can be placed without using king logic
  • Easy — kings are necessary to solve the full puzzle but are still fairly trivial
  • Medium — more complex interaction between kings and queens
  • Hard — long deduction chains, set logic
  • Expert — deep chains, king pairing traps, cascade reasoning
