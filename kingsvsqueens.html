👑 How to Play

Fill the board by placing 8 Queens and 2 Kings.

♛ Queens

  • Place one queen in each colored region (estate).
  • No two queens may share the same row or column.
  • Queens cannot be adjacent to each other (including diagonals) — but they can share a long diagonal.

♚ Kings

  • Both kings go on gray cells only.
  • Kings cannot be adjacent to or on the same diagonal as any queen (at any distance).
  • Kings cannot share a row, column, or diagonal with each other.

Controls

  • Tap an empty cell → place a cross (✕) to mark it eliminated.
  • Double-tap a cross → upgrade to ♛ (on color) or ♚ (on gray).
  • Hold a placed piece → highlight its attack zones.
  • Drag across empty cells → place crosses quickly.
💡 Start with queens: look for estates with very few options, or rows/columns where only one estate fits. Use crosses to eliminate impossible cells, then narrow down to the solution!
