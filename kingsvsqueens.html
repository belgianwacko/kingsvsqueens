How to Play

Place 8 Queens and 2 Kings on an 8×8 grid. Each puzzle has exactly one solution.

Queens

  • One queen per colored estate
  • No two queens in the same row or column
  • Queens cannot be adjacent (including diagonals)
  • Queens can share a long diagonal

Kings

  • Both kings on gray cells only
  • No king adjacent to or on the same diagonal as a queen (any distance)
  • Kings cannot share a row, column, or diagonal

Controls

  • Tap → place cross ✕
  • Double-tap → place queen ♛ or king ♚
  • Hold → show attack zones
  • Drag → multi-cross
